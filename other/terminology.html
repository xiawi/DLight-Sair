Terminology

There are quite a few moves that we can do in Brawlhalla, and in order to keep track of how inputs should be performed, a proper understanding of terminology is required. Terminology in Brawlhalla is rather simple, and it shouldn't take more than 30 minutes to get the hang of everything.

Neutral Light (NLight)

A Neutral Light is achieved by performing a light attack without holding any directional keys.

Side Light (SLight)

A Side Light is achieved by performing a light attack while holding either the left or right key.

Down Light (DLight)

A Down Light is achieved by performing a light attack while holding the down key.

Neutral Signature / Neutral Heavy (NSig)

A Neutral Signature is achieved by performing a heavy attack without holding any directional keys.

Side Signature / Side Heavy (SSig)

A Side Signature is achieved by performing a heavy attack while holding either the left or right key.

Down Signature / Down Heavy (DSig)

A Down Signature is achieved by performing a heavy attack while holding the down key.

Neutral Air (Nair)

A Neutral Air is achieved by performing a light attack without holding any directional keys when the legend model is in the air.

Side Air (Sair)

A Side Air is achieved by performing a light attack while holding either the left or right key when the legend model is in the air.

Down Air (Dair)

A Down Air is achieved by performing a light attack while holding the down key when the legend model is in the air.

Recovery (Rec)

A Recovery is achieved by performing a heavy attack while the legend model is in the air. A recovery can be performed while holding any directional key except the down key.

Ground Pound (GP)

A Ground Pound is achieved by performing a heavy attack while holding the down key when the legend model is in the air.

Chase Dodge (CD)

A Chase Dodge is achieved by performing a directional dodge towards an opponent right after landing a hit on the opponent.

Gravity Cancel (GC)

A Gravity Cancel is when a player performs a grounded move in the air. This is achieved by performing a neutral dodge in the air, followed immediately by the desired grounded attack.
