Yumiko

Stats

Strength 4
Dexterity 7
Defense 4
Speed 7

Yumiko is one of the more unique legends in the Brawlhalla roster. Some of her signatures have lingering hitboxes. Due to the fact that she doesn't function like the rest of the roster, she has a small but dedicated playerbase. Yumiko carries a hammer as well as a bow.

Signatures

Bow NSig

Yumiko turns herself into a ball of fire and shoots herself diagonally upwards

Yumiko's bow NSig is a useful tool to K.O. floaty oppoonents.

Bow SSig

Yumiko turns herself into a ball of fire and shoots herself forward.

Yumiko's bow SSig, like her bow Nsig, is very good at K.O.ing opponents early.

Bow DSig

Yumiko jumps backwards, throwing out three fireballs in a line with lingering hitboxes.

This is one of the two more unique signatures in Yumiko's kit, players can combo out of Yumiko's DSig with almost any aerial bow move.

Hammer NSig

Yumiko jumps and does a side flip. If she catches an opponent, she sends them downwards forcefully.

Yumiko's hammer NSig is useful for edge-guard situations (when opponents are off-stage and you're on stage).

Hammer SSig

Yumiko dashes forward with immense speed before stopping herself by rotating her body.

Yumiko's hammer SSig is kind of versatile; depending on where you hit the signature, the opposing player could fly forwards or backwards.

Hammer DSig

Yumiko stays in place as she fires off three balls of fire diagnoally above her.

Another unique signature, the lingering hitboxes can make it frustrating for a player to get back to stage.
