Hattori

Stats

Strength 4
Dexterity 6
Defense 4
Speed 8

Hattori is a legend that's very beginner friendly, she carries a sword and spear with her; Her high speed allows her to make riskier plays and go for off-stage kills more.

Signatures

Sword NSig

Hattori essentially teleports upwards and does a powerful downward slice. This move sends the opponent flying diagonally downwards.

Hattori's sword NSig is especially useful in edge-guarding situations (when the opponent is off-stage and you're on stage)

Sword SSig

Hattori quickly swings her sword in front of her. If the first swing hits an opponent, she sends them flying backwards with a second swing.

Hattori's sword SSig is relatively quick and can be used to punish opponents who like throwing out attacks that have many active frames.

Sword DSig

Hattori performs a back-handspring and quickly dashes forward to strike the opponent. This move sends the opponent diagonally upwards.

Hattori's sword DSig can be used to punish opponents who over aggress, the back-handspring allows you to gain extra distance before striking.

Spear NSig

Hattori's hitbox momentarily goes "under" before she appears a few feet in front of where she was, poking the opponent from underneath, sending them upwards.

Hattori's spear NSig can be used to K.O. opponents who play too floaty.

Spear SSig

Hattori vaults forward using her spear and kicks her opponent.

Hattori's spear SSig is much like her sword's it's relatively quick and can catch the unsuspecting player off guard.

Spear DSig

Hattori covers herself in a smoke screen before striking, sending the opponent forward.

Hattori's spear DSig is the most unique signature in her kit. It can be useful in maps with a shenanigans area, or against players who like to dodge in.
